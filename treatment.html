TREATMENT ON COVID 19 PATIENTS

  • Isolation (such as quarantine, but for those with COVID-19 symptoms) and social incarceration (where people without symptoms maintain a physical distance from each other)
    •               
  • Malaysia's health ministry will continue to use hydroxychloroquine in trials to treat COVID-19 patients, its director-general Noor Hisham Abdullah said on Tuesday (May 26), adding that authorities will closely monitor any side effects.
    •               

  • If the patient's symptoms are severe such as shortness of breath due to lack of oxygen, supportive care through oxygen delivery should be provided to the patient.
    •               
  • The patient's own immune system will act to produce antibodies to kill the virus and the patient will heal on its own.
    •               
  • For now, no specific antiviral treatment has been used to treat the disease. Patients with COVID-19 disease should be given supportive treatment to reduce symptoms

SYMPTOMS OF COVID-19

The virus can lead to pneumonia, respiratory failure, septic shock and death. Many Covid-19 complications may be caused by a condition known as cytokine release syndrome or a cytokine storm.

This is when an infection triggers your immune system to flood your bloodstream with inflammatory proteins called cytokines. They can kill tissue and damage your organs.

COVID-19 affects different people in different ways. Most infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization.

1. Most common symptoms:-
  • fever
  • dry cough
  • tiredness

2. Less common symptoms:-
  • aches and pains
  • sore throat
  • diarrhoea
  • conjunctivitis
  • headache
  • loss of taste or smell
  • a rash on skin, or discolouration of fingers or toes

3. Serious symptoms:-
  • difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  • chest pain or pressure
  • loss of speech or movement

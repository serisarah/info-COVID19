WHAT IS COVID 19???

- A coronavirus is a kind of common virus that causes an infection in your noses, sinuses or upper throat. Most coronaviruses aren't dangerous.

- In early 2020, after a December 2019 outbreak in China, the World Heath Organization identified SARS-CoV-2 as a new type of coronavirus. The outbreak quickly spread around the world.

- Covid 19 is a disease caused by SARS-CoV-2 that can trigger what doctors call a respiratory tract infection. It can affect your upper respiratory tract (sinuses, nose and throat) or lower repiratory tract (windpipe and lungs).

- It spreads the same way other coronaviruses do, mainly through person-to-person contact. Infections range from mild to deadly.

WAYS TO PREVENT THE SPREAD OF COVID 19

1. Ways to prevent the spread of COVID-19:-

  • Clean your hands often. Use soap and water, or an alcohol-based hand rub.
  • Maintain a safe distance from anyone who is coughing or sneezing.
  • Wear a mask when physical distancing is not possible.
  • Donâ€™t touch your eyes, nose or mouth.
  • Cover your nose and mouth with your bent elbow or a tissue when you cough or sneeze.
  • Stay home if you feel unwell.
  • If you have a fever, cough and difficulty breathing, seek medical attention.
  • Always call before visiting your doctor or health facility.


2. What should you do if you just come home from abroad?

  • You can work normally if you do not have any symptoms.
  • There are some companies that give their employees 14 days' leave when they return from the country. But this depends on the discretion of your workplace.
  • If you have symptoms within 14 days after arriving in Malaysia, you should go to the nearest health facility.
  • You should also tell the staff at the front of the facility that you have a history of going to the affected country and having the symptoms.
